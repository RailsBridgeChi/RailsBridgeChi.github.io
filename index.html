RailsBridge started in San Francisco in 2009 as a project to increase diversity in the Ruby community. And it’s been wildly successful! The past six years have seen dozens of local chapters form, thousands of students learn to code, hundreds of volunteers donate their time, and a lot of new developer careers launch!
